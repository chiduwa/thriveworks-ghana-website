Vocational and entrepreneurial skills training for Ghana's youth

We train young people ages 15 to 25 in Sunyani, Bono Region, and the Ahafo host communities, then help them find real local work, start a business, or continue their education.

ThriveWorks Ghana

ThriveWorks Ghana is an NGO based in Sunyani, Bono Region, serving youth across the Bono and Ahafo host communities. We work with young people ages 15 to 25 who are facing the same hard problem: few stable jobs and few affordable ways to learn a trade. Our response is hands-on apprenticeship and mentorship training, plus basic entrepreneurship education, so graduates can find local employment, start a small business, or continue their education.

Mission, Vision, & Values

Vision

To create a capable, credible, and thriving community where everyone has the opportunity to develop their God-given talents and be valuable contributors to society.

Mission

To equip underserved youth in Ghana with the skills, attitudes, and mindset they need to be Leaders who are gainfully employed and achieve success in their careers.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Innovation
  • Service
  • Excellence

Our Programs

Our programs aim to offer different career opportunities for young people in the country who are currently facing high rates of unemployment or underemployment. Additionally, we believe work brings dignity and independence while it enables an individual to support others. Because of our values, we go beyond just the skills training to help our beneficiaries build their character and value integrity in work and personal lives.

We partner with local churches to use their space for training and mentoring, since that keeps our costs down and puts programs in places young people already know. Everyone is welcome regardless of background or religious affiliation.

Model

Most of our programs are executed by leveraging existing resources and relationships to build our network of trainers. Together, we establish curriculum and expectations/standards between beneficiaries & trainers. We then provide funding & resources necessary for the training while we monitor and evaluate the process to ensure desired outcomes.
